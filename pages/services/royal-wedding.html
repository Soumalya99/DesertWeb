Royal Weddings in the Golden Sands

Where Love Meets Legacy

Contact For Details

Celebrate your special day in the majestic embrace of the Thar Desert, where every moment feels like a scene from a royal fairytale. At our desert camp in Sam, Jaisalmer, we specialize in crafting unforgettable Royal Wedding Experiences—infused with regal Rajasthani charm, golden dunes, and timeless traditions.

Imagine saying your vows under the open sky, with the golden sunset as your backdrop and folk melodies floating through the air. Whether it's an intimate celebration or a grand affair, our team ensures every detail is as majestic as the dunes themselves.

What We Offer:

  • Royal-themed decor with traditional Rajasthani elegance
  • Luxury tented accommodation for guests
  • Desert-themed mandap & sunset pheras
  • Camel and vintage jeep processions for baraat
  • Folk music, dance performances, and fire shows
  • Custom catering with authentic Rajasthani and multi-cuisine menus
  • Full wedding planning & coordination services

Our vast desert landscape transforms into a private, magical setting—perfect for making memories that last a lifetime. Make your wedding a royal chapter in the sands of time.
